Frequently Asked Questions

How is the WhatsApp verification done?

We query WhatsApp's contact discovery protocol directly, which tells us whether a phone number is registered on WhatsApp. This is the same mechanism WhatsApp uses to show which of your contacts are on the platform. We then cross-verify with carrier HLR lookup to confirm the line itself is active.

Do you store or resell the numbers?

No. Each data order is freshly verified. We don't maintain a static database that gets stale. Numbers are verified at the time of your order, ensuring maximum freshness. Data is delivered exclusively to you and never resold.

What format is the data delivered in?

CSV format with columns: phone_number (E.164), country_code, carrier, account_type (personal/business), verification_timestamp, status. Custom formats (JSON, Excel, SQL) available on request.
