Frequently Asked Questions

How are Facebook-linked phone numbers detected?

We verify Facebook account linkage by querying Facebook's contact discovery and account registration signals. Each number is then validated through carrier HLR lookup to confirm it's on an active phone line. We detect whether the number is linked to a personal profile, Facebook Page, or both.

Can I filter Facebook users by interest or behavior?

Basic filtering is available by country, profile type (personal vs page), and post activity level. Advanced interest-based or behavioral targeting beyond these dimensions is not available to protect user privacy and comply with platform policies.

Is Facebook data compliant with platform policies?

Our data is sourced from publicly available signals and verified through carrier-level checks. We do not scrape Facebook, access non-public profile data, or violate Facebook's Terms of Service. We recommend reviewing your campaign's compliance with Facebook's advertising and data policies.
