Frequently Asked Questions

How is Telegram number verification done?

We query Telegram's contact discovery API to confirm registration status. This is the same mechanism Telegram uses to let you know which of your contacts are on the platform. We combine this with carrier HLR lookup to confirm the underlying phone line is active — not a virtual or disconnected number.

What activity data do you include?

We classify users by last seen recency: active (within 24h), recent (within 1 week), occasional (within 1 month), and dormant (1+ month). We also flag Telegram Premium subscribers. Activity data is based on publicly observable signals available through Telegram's API.

Can I get Telegram usernames with the data?

Telegram username/profile data is available as an optional add-on for select countries. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements — availability varies by region due to privacy settings.

What format is the data delivered in?

CSV format with columns: phone_number (E.164), country_code, carrier, telegram_status (active/inactive), activity_level, premium_flag, verification_timestamp. Custom formats available on request.
