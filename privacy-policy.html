Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

888 Number Check (numbercheck.live) provides free phone number verification tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our services.

Our core principle: we do not store, log, or retain phone numbers you check. Every lookup is processed in real-time and the number is discarded immediately after verification.

2. Information We Process

Phone Numbers You Check: When you enter a phone number into our tools (WhatsApp checker, Telegram checker, format validator, etc.), the number is transmitted directly to carrier APIs and app platform APIs for verification. The number is not stored on our servers, not written to any database, and not retained in any logs.

No Personal Account Data: We do not require registration, login, or account creation. We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment information for our free tools.

3. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand site traffic and usage patterns. GA4 collects anonymized data including:

  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)
  • Browser type and device category
  • Referral source (how you found us)

GA4 does not track individual users or collect personally identifiable information. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Third-Party Services

Our verification tools query third-party APIs (carrier networks, WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) to determine number registration status. These queries contain only the phone number — no additional user or device information is transmitted.

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare CDN. Both may collect standard server logs (IP address, request time, user agent) for operational purposes. These logs are not accessible to us for individual identification.

5. Data Security

All connections to numbercheck.live are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We follow security best practices including:

  • No storage of phone numbers or verification results on any persistent storage
  • All API communications use encrypted channels
  • No user-identifiable data is collected or retained
  • Static site deployment minimizes attack surface

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or process information from children.
