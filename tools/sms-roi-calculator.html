This calculator uses industry benchmarks to estimate how much you're spending on numbers that will never receive your message:

Hidden Costs of Bad Phone Data

The wasted per-message cost is just the tip of the iceberg. Invalid numbers in your list create cascading costs:

  • Sender reputation damage — High bounce rates flag your sender ID with carriers. Repeatedly hitting invalid numbers can get your messages throttled or blocked entirely.
  • Skewed analytics — If 25% of your sends hit dead air, your open rates, CTR, and conversion metrics are all understated. You're making decisions on bad data.
  • Compliance risk — Sending messages to recycled numbers that now belong to someone else can trigger spam complaints and TCPA/GDPR issues.
  • Opportunity cost — The slot a bad number occupies in your campaign could have gone to a valid prospect. Every invalid send is a missed opportunity to convert.
  • Team time waste — Sales teams following up on undelivered messages to dead numbers wastes hours that could be spent on real prospects.
