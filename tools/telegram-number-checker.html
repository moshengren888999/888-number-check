Telegram is one of the world's largest messaging platforms with over 900 million active users. Our Telegram number checker queries Telegram's official registration interfaces to determine whether a phone number has an active Telegram account.

Unlike third-party databases that may be outdated, our detection is real-time — it checks the current registration status at the moment of query.
