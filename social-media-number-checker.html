Frequently Asked Questions

Can I check if a phone number is on Facebook?

Yes. Our detection engine queries Facebook's registration API to check if a phone number is associated with a Facebook account. Combined with Instagram (owned by Meta), you can verify presence across the Meta ecosystem. Note: Facebook's privacy settings may limit detection for accounts with phone number visibility disabled.

Can I find someone's Instagram by phone number?

Our tool detects whether a phone number is registered on Instagram, but it does not reveal the Instagram username or profile. It's a presence check, not an identity reveal. This respects user privacy while giving you the signal you need: is this number on Instagram or not?
